How it works

Up and running in under a minute.

1

Launch the Server

Download and run OpenDropServer on your Windows PC. The server starts automatically and creates a secure tunnel.

2

Scan the QR Code

Open the OpenDrop mobile app and scan the QR code displayed on your computer. Connection is instant.

3

Transfer Files

Browse files on your PC from your phone, or send files from your phone to your computer. Fast, simple, done.
